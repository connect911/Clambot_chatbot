How to Sign Up

Follow these steps to create your account:

  1. Go to the landing page and click the "Get Started" button.
  2. You will be redirected to the signup form. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password).
  3. Click the "Submit" button to complete the signup process.

Email Verification

After signing up, you need to verify your email:

  1. Check your email inbox for a verification email from our website.
  2. Open the email and click on the verification link.
  3. You will be redirected to the login page of the website.

How to Log In

Once your email is verified, you can log in to your account:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Enter your registered email and password.
  3. Click the "Login" button to access the platform.

Using the Chat Interface

After logging in, you can use the chat interface to communicate:

  1. Navigate to the chat page from the dashboard.
  2. Type your message in the input box and press Enter to send.
  3. Receive responses in real-time and continue the conversation.
